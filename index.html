A Guide to Adventure Time Characters

A Spoiler-Free Introduction to Adventure Time

Adventure Time is an animated TV show following the adventures of two best friends (Finn and Jake) in the Land of Ooo. Fighting evil, making friends, tackling boredom, and taking on any task that comes their way, Finn and Jake navigate life.

In the show as a whole, there are 5 main characters:
  • Finn the Human
  • Jake the Dog
  • Princess Bubblegum
  • Ice King

Finn the Human

Finn Mertens is a 12-year-old boy that craves adventure and protecting the citizens of the surrounding kingdoms. Seemingly the only human in all of Ooo, Finn was abandoned by his father as a baby. Finn was later found and adopted by Jake the Dog's family. Finn often puts himself in the path of danger for the sake of helping others. In addition to his physical combat abilities, Finn also practiced as a wizard. Finn's hobbies are making music, adventuring, and playing video games. His main character traits are bravery, selflessness, integrity, creativity, and kindness.

Jake the Dog

A magical yellow dog, Jake is Finn's best friend and adoptive brother. Jake was a criminal, but changed his ways once Finn was introduced into his life. Jake is able to morph his body into an endless combination of shapes an sizes. His elasticity, creative problem-solving, and strength allow Jake to adapt to nearly any situation. Jakes hobbies include adventuring, playing video games, and making music with the viola. Jake eventually has five puppies with his partner, Lady Rainicorn, (a rainbow-unicorn hybrid): Charlie, Jake Jr., T.V., Viola, and Kim Kil Whan. His main character traits include dependability, loyalty, stubbornness and sensitivity.

Princess Bubblegum

Princess Bubblegum is the ruler of the Candy Kingdom. Physically composed of bubblegum herself, she is as sweet as she is strong. She is in charge of all the candy people within Ooo. She is a brilliant scientist who is often found in her lab making potions that will benefit Finn and Jake's exploration. Her intelligence, concern for others, and genuine heart help the Candy Kingdom thrive. Her main capabilities include strong leadership, logical-thinking, level-headedness, and combat proficiency.
